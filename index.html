Invisible Shadows

The Feast

Pre-Production
Director: Ryan Christian
Humanity, unknowingly, has been nothing more than a carefully tended crop, destined to become nourishment for these long-dormant and forgotten creatures. As the feast begins, survival becomes a desperate struggle, but escape is just an illusion. This is not a hunt, but a reaping, and with each devoured life, the last vestiges of hope fade away, leaving humanity to face the chilling reality of its true place in the cosmic order.
